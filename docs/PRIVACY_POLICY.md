# Privacy Policy for WebNote

**Effective Date:** [Heutiges Datum eintragen, z.B. 28. April 2026]

This Privacy Policy explains how the "WebNote" browser extension ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
WebNote is designed with strict privacy in mind. **We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.** 

All data generated by your use of the extension—including but not limited to:
- Text notes
- Highlighted texts
- Freehand drawings, lines, and shapes
- Toolbar preferences and positions
- URL associations

is stored **locally** on your device using the browser's native `chrome.storage.local` API.

## 2. Third-Party Access
Because all data remains exclusively on your personal device, we do not share, sell, or rent your data to any third parties. Your notes and web annotations are completely private to you.

## 3. Permissions Explained
To function properly, WebNote requests the following browser permissions. These are used strictly locally on your machine:
- **Storage:** To save your notes and annotations so they persist when you revisit a webpage.
- **ActiveTab / All URLs:** To inject the drawing canvas and note UI exclusively on the webpages you actively visit and choose to annotate.
- **Scripting:** To run the local code necessary to render notes and drawings over the webpage content.
- **ContextMenus:** To provide you with quick right-click actions for creating notes or highlighting text.

## 4. Data Deletion
You are in full control of your data. You can delete individual notes, clear highlights, or erase drawings directly within the extension's user interface. If you uninstall the WebNote extension, all associated local data stored by the extension will be automatically and permanently deleted by your browser.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with a new "Effective Date." Because we do not collect your contact information, we encourage you to review this policy periodically in the Chrome Web Store.
